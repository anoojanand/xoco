About Xoco

Like all discerning chefs, we begin with superior ingredients, including the world’s finest chocolate, and in reverence to flavor we leave out the preservatives. The artistry of chocolate means everything to us at Xoco. Then we proceed with an uncommon blend of creativity and precision to transform simple but wonderful ingredients into true culinary works of art. The result is excellence in every detail, in every chocolate we produce.

Creator of unique tastes

Chocolate is our love and our passion. Xoco is dedicated to creating some of the world’s most exquisite chocolate through traditional techniques.

We source only the world’s very best beans and ingredients. We visit the plantations, buy from the growers, and when necessary work with them to improve their skill in properly growing, fermenting and drying the cocoa beans to meet our exacting standards.

Through working with small, carefully controlled batches and lots of love and attention, we seek not to be the largest chocolate company, simply to make the very best chocolate humanly — or heavenly — possible.
